Our Cupcakes

Seamlessly fusing the best of a trendy and sophisticated New York coffee lounge with everybody's childhood fantasy of decadent desserts , LA Sweetz sets the gold standard for a new generation of hotspots. LA Sweetz is Miami's meccah of elegant desserts. Using only the finest, freshest ingredients, all cupcakes are baked daily and piped with real buttercream frosting. Incredibly moist and delicate, each cupcake is an exquisite blend of luscious flavors. Owner and baker Letty Alvarez, pours profound passion into every batch of cupcakes and every whip of buttercream. Committed to consistency and excellence in quality, LA Sweetz also aims to provide superlative service to every dessert connoisseur.
